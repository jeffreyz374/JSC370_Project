Miscellaneous Interactive Visuals

Xiaotang (Jeffrey) Zhou


These are additional interactive visuals that are derived from the information on all of the goals ever scored by Ovechkin and Gretzky. Although not relevant to the scope of the project, these visuals still provide a comparison between the individual goal scoring talents of both players.

Note that as Ovechkin is currently an active player in the NHL and the 2023-24 NHL Regular Season is currently ongoing, any data corresponding to him is accurate as of the last update to this website. This update was most recently performed on March 8th 2024.



Ovechkin and Gretzky Goals By Age

This interactive visual shows the number of goals scored by Ovechkin and Gretzky grouped by the age at which each player scored those goals.
